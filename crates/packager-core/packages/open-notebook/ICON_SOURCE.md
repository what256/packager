# Open Notebook icon

`icon.icns` was generated from Open Notebook's official `logo.png`, and
`icon.ico` is the official favicon from the Open Notebook repository:

- https://github.com/lfnovo/open-notebook/blob/main/logo.png
- https://github.com/lfnovo/open-notebook/blob/main/frontend/src/app/favicon.ico

Open Notebook is distributed under the MIT license. The icon files remain
Open Notebook project assets; their inclusion here identifies the packaged app.
